Why AutoShare?

⚡ Auto Accept

Once you pair devices, files are automatically accepted. No need to click approve every time.

🔒 100% Local

Files are transferred over your local Wi-Fi. Completely private, fast, and does not consume internet quota.

🤝 Simple Pairing

Connect devices using a secure token-based pairing system. Setup once, share forever.

🔞 Play Age Safety

Integrated Play Age Signals on Android to verify parental consent and secure supervised accounts.
